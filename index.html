ABOUT

Built for the real restaurant floor.

ScannDine was built from the ground up to solve the actual friction points in restaurant service — long wait times, miscommunicated orders, and tangled bills.

By connecting every role — guest, kitchen, admin, and cashier — through one real-time platform, we help restaurants serve faster, smarter, and with far fewer mistakes.

  • Zero app installs required
  • Works with any existing workflow
  • Built on Node.js, Socket.io & PostgreSQL
  • Real-time updates across all devices
